# How to Add USDC to Your Freighter Wallet

Before you can create jobs with USDC payment, you need to add the USDC token to your Freighter wallet.

## Steps:

### 1. Open Freighter Wallet

Click on the Freighter extension in your browser

### 2. Go to "Manage Assets"

- Click on the three dots menu (â‹®) in the top right
- Select "Manage Assets"

### 3. Add USDC Token

- Click "Add Asset" or "Add Token"
- Select "Add Manually" or "Custom Asset"

### 4. Enter USDC Contract Address

```
CBIELTK6YBZJU5UP2WWQEUCYKLPU6AUNZ2BQ4WWFEIE3USCIHMXQDAMA
```

### 5. Confirm

- Click "Add" or "Confirm"
- You should now see USDC in your asset list with a balance of 0

### 6. Get Test USDC (Optional)

For testing, you can get free test USDC from the faucet or contact the project admin.
